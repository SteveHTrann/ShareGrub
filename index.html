Lets share food



We aim to raise the level of compassion within a community by increasing accessibility to food. As a byproduct, this program also raises awareness of food insecurities issues within a community to increase the call to action to tackle the problem.

We provide a simplistic and streamlined way for retailers with potential food waste to connect with individuals in need of food.
