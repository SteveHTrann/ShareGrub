About Us

This website allows retailers to connect with and donate food to individuals who are food insecure.
By doing so, we will be able to:

  1. Reduce food waste
  2. Lower greenhouse gas emissions
  3. Reduce hunger

while providing a carbon offset incentive to retailers.

Key Values of our Program

Wellbeing

1 in 6 children in the United States lives in a food-insecure household
We help to reduce the food insecurity in the community by provinding good quality food to the local community for free

Environment Justice

The U.S. wastes up to 40% of food every year.
Using this app, we can help reduce food waste by encouraging them to donate food to local people and earn carbon points.

Civic Enagegement

We encourage students wanting some volunteering hours or any member in the comunity to sign up as volnteers in the website and help deliver groceries from the store to the local community.

Our story

When Paige and her classmates were given a project on food security, they made the decision to put themselves in the position of someone who experiences food insecurity. They looked into various ways how people experiencing food insecurity take to assure their next meal. They discovered that one typical strategy for finding food was dumpster diving.

They set out in the middle of the night to see how difficult it was to obtain enough food to fill their stomachs, expecting to only discover rotting food scraps. To their surprise, they arrived with numerous bags of fresh produce with minor dents and bruises, which were compeletely edible. With what they had acquired, they were able to prepare a whole meal for their class. It was extremely heartbreaking to find that much edible food was being wasted, increasing greenhouse gas emissions, while 1 in 6 Americans struggled to eat.

ShareGrub aims to address both the massive amount of food discarded by restaurants and grocery stores, while also returning dignity and agency to those suffering from food insecurity. No person should have to jump to a dumpster for their next meal!
